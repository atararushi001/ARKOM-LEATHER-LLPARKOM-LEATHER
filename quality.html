Quality & R&D

‘Arkom’ is very strict and adhered to the quality control and assurance. The quality control standards adopted by ‘Arkom’ have broadened the horizons for its product. A team specially designed to closely monitor each stage of the process to reduce the wastages, defects and deviation in customer’s specification and requirement. Arkom Group is now all set to have online process monitoring, record keeping, and maintaining.

Our Key Initiatives Include:

  • Continuous improvement of product design and functionality
  • Adoption of cutting-edge technology and processes
  • Collaboration with industry experts and researchers
  • Regular quality audits and inspections

'Arkom' have preplanned quality for each raw material used to manufacture finished goods. Each incoming raw material passes through stringent monitoring process where any deviation in the specified raw materials is checked and monitored. After each test is satisfied by comparing the test results with internal quality and supplier’s report, material is sent for storage. ‘Roseli’ has a policy which mentions that the raw material can only be bought from a standard supplier to reduce rejection at the first stage.
