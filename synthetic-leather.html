Description

It is the world of faux leather! The synthetic leather or artificial leather also named as rexine, vinyl, or leatherette has varied uses. This man-made fabric uses PVC or polyurethane that is treated and dyed so that it resembles near perfect to the real leather. Whereas the real leather is costly and not used due to ethical reasons, artificial leather is a perfect substitute that is way cheaper than the real leather and more versatile too.
