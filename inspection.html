Inspection

Inspection Of Incoming Material:

  • Also called incoming inspection. It consists of checking and verifying all raw materials (chemicals, resins, plasticizers, pigments, substances, etc.) purchased. Our quality team then checks all incoming raw materials thoroughly and ensures that they are in order for further processing. If the raw material does not meet our standards, it will be rejected and sent back to the supplier. We have a fully equipped laboratory for testing and checking the incoming material, consisting of devices such as Brookfield Viscometers, Hydrometers, Grind meters, Karl Fischer Titrometers, Coater- Laboratory, bar coater, ovens, fabric yarn counters, tensile tester, etc.

Online Inspection:

  • The online inspection consists of reviewing and approving the first part according to customer specifications and our internal specifications during production. It is an important step in ensuring that the raw material is adequate according to internal and external specifications. Colour, feel, weight, thickness, finish and all other mandatory requirements are verified before proceeding with further production. Tensile Tester, Vessel Gauge, Spectrophotometer, Bend Tester, Abrasion Tester, Thickness Gauge, Scales are some of the devices this laboratory uses for the control process.

Our Inspection Process Includes:

  • Visual Inspection: Examining products for visible defects and inconsistencies.
  • Functional Testing: Ensuring that products perform as intended under various conditions.
  • Durability Testing: Assessing the longevity and robustness of products.
  • Compliance Checks: Verifying that products meet industry standards and regulations.
