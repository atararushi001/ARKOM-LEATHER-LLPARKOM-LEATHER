CSR

“'With great power create with greater responsibility.'’

Corporate social responsibility (CSR) is the idea that businesses should operate according to principles and policies that make a positive impact on society and the environment. Going beyond corporate philanthropy we have a well-developed CSR model. Situated in Gujarat, which is economically a Golden state in India, the value generation that we do through our CSR programs is commendable.

The whole organization is sensitive and dedicated to the vision of creating an organization whose name outlives its functioning. Natural resource conservation and CO2 emission regulation in the manufacturing process makes us a forward-looking organization, but what makes us stand out from other industries is the impeccable contribution made in CSR.

Through CSR, companies make decisions driven by financial gain and profitability, and the impact of their actions on their communities and the world at large. CSR goes beyond legal obligations: by voluntarily adopting ethical, sustainable and responsible business practices, companies seek to deliver benefits to consumers, shareholders, employees and society.

WHY US?

We are manufacturing, exporting and supplier of artificial leather, synthetic leather for cloth, fashion garments, furnishing, shoes, coated fabric, rexine leather and other leather accessories.

ARKOM LEATHER is a leading manufacturer of artificial leather and synthetic leather products based in Morbi and Rajkot, Gujarat, India. We are committed to providing high-quality, innovative, and sustainable leather alternatives for a variety of applications. Our designers create products that adhere to established industry standards using high-quality raw materials and cutting-edge technology. The range of products we offer is highly regarded in the market for its impeccable finish, resistance to shrinkage, soft feel, and appealing design. we offer our product range in a variety of designs, colors, patterns, and sizes at affordable prices.
